Description

Usra is dedicated to empowering women in Saudi Arabia with secure, private legal services. Our platform connects you to expert consultants for family, personal, and urgent legal matters, always with respect and care.

We provide guidance on marriage, divorce, custody, inheritance, abuse, and more. Usra ensures every woman can access legal help safely, with bilingual support and a user-friendly experience.

Answers to Your Usra Questions

Usra is here to make legal help simple and accessible for women. Find answers to common questions about our services and how we protect your privacy.

What services does Usra provide for women?

Usra offers private legal consultations, family and personal law support, emergency help, document preparation, and women's rights education—all tailored for Saudi women.

How do I book a consultation with Usra?

You can schedule a consultation by contacting us through our website’s contact form, giving us a call, or sending an email. Our team will promptly respond to arrange a convenient time for your consultation.

What should I bring to my initial consultation?

For your first meeting, we recommend bringing any relevant documents related to your case, such as contracts, legal notices, or any other pertinent information. This helps us better understand your situation and offer more informed advice.
